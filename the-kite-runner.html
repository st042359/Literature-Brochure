The Kite Runner

Author: Khaled Hosseini
Published in 2003

The Kite Runner, is a novel , written by Khaled Hosseini, tells the extraordinary story of an unlikely friendship between a wealthy Afghan boy, named Amir, and Hassan, the son of his father's servant — who he later finds out, after Hassan's death, has always been his half-brother.
